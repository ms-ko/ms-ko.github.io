About

Hi! I am a 4th year Ph.D. student in Computer Engineering at Virginia Tech, fortunately working under the supervision of Prof. Ruoxi Jia. Prior to joining Virginia Tech, I received my M.S. (2018) and B.S. (2016) in Electronics and Information Engineering from Korea University.

I am deeply interested in exploring and understanding machine learning problems. Recently, I have been studying unlearning problems for foundation models. I am also interested in machine learning problems through the lens of data.
